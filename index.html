FAQ

Quick answers—so you don’t have to call.

Do I have to call to sign up?

Nope. Use the form below to request service and we’ll confirm details by text or email—your choice.

Is recycling included?

Recycling is treated like an additional can. Any trash, recycling, or yard-waste bin counts as one can.

What if my driveway is long?

Our pricing assumes a standard residential driveway. If yours is unusually long or has special access needs, we’ll send a quick custom quote.

How does monthly can cleaning work?

Premium includes one cleaning per month. We’ll need access to your water source for the cleaning service.
